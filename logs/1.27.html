However, the entire realm lacks the usual stench and ozone charge of the warp, instead appearing to be an interstitial zone of reality. Most of this I am sure you already know from other reports but I record here for the sake of protocol. Myrkr's intent is hard to discern once again, but from what we can gather the fleet is attempting to siphon biomass from each of these World Shards back to their core fleet to reconstitute the losses they sustained after the Trivilus incident. We have been unable to determine if the adaptation from what we have typically identified as Hive Fleet Leviathan to what we believe to be a strain of Gorgon was caused by the initial breach into this interstitial zone. Regarding World Shard: WS.YHP-724, we were originally drawn towards this shard pursuing Myrkr. As they began their biomass harvesting it appears they aggravated a local Drukkhari raiding group that must have been pulled in during the breach. This World Shard appears to have once been an old Imperial Industrial world and somehow, beyond belief, some of the local citizens survived the transition. Observing these citizens initially brought on a sense of unease uncharacterstic to our kind, but the initial feeling would not become clear until later. We can only conclude that the raiding group must have been hunting for live targets as well, though for more... depraved means. The xenos engaged each other and tore through most of the remaining ruins. It appears that, while a close battle, the hive fleet fell to the raiding party. This victory was obtained at great cost it appears, as the tyranids were able to siphon off significant biomass from the enemy and world shard in the process. What was curious is what occurred next. Only through careful review of our auspex readings and meditation to re-orient my mind was I able to discern what had occurred. It appears that this world shard is under a temporal loop. Shortly after their initial engagement, it appears that everything had reset to it's initial state. Citizens previously abducted or consumed were returned, ruins were rebuilt, and any signs of battle erased. Both xenos forces then engaged in the same conflict. What is curious about this is that each force clearly had grown or retained scars from the previous engagement, just did not appear to have any recollection that they had fought before. The composition of each force had varied slightly between egagements, but not in any significant way to indicate that either force retained previous knowledge of their prior engagement. The fleet was victorious in the second bout and appeared to be attempting to terraform the world shard with a Parasite organism. The raiding party seemed focused on desecrating key points across the battlefield, which at first appeared to have no effect, but once the leader organisms were brought down turned out to have a detrimental effect on the lesser organisms. I wouldn't categorize it as fear as such, but rather some sort of psychic dampening similar to our Anathema Psykana cohorts. This in conjunction with a lack of synaptic oversight appeared to be enough to further deteriorate the temperament of the lesser organisms. After both xenos forces left the world shard, everything returned to its prior state. It is difficult to report on this shard without great concentration due to an insistence in my mind that the world shard was always in that state. Each time I look upon these ruins and the few survivors within it my mind is in conflict trying to reconcile the reality that this place was ravaged. A primary concern regarding WS.YHP-274 and these specific xenos forces is that with it's ability to reset yet still provide resources to both xenos forces, this could be used by either as an endless battery of sorts, providing each enemy force with critical strategic gains without losing anything substantial in the process. However, given the nature of this shard to alter the temporal perceptions of any who observe it I do not think that any attempt to exploit this anomoly in this manner will arise deliberately. If there is any indication of a deliberate intent to do so, I fear we must respond in order to prevent it.
